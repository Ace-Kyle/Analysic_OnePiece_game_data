About Character Rankings

Welcome to the ONE PIECE Bounty Rush Character Rankings! This page shows the most popular and highest-scoring characters based on actual game getData. Rankings are updated at the end of each season.

The ranking calculation takes into account:

  • Average points earned by top 500 players for each character
  • Special detection system to filter out auto-clickers and other abnormal getData
  • Adjustment for newly released characters with fewer players

Use the filters below to sort by role, element, or rarity to find specific characters!
